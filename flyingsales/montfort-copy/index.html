Our ethics and compliance framework

At Flying Sales, we operate under an integrated Sustainability Framework and adhere to strict corporate governance principles that allow us drive transformative social and environmental progress.

We ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations across our global operations, including those of the UN, EU, Switzerland, UK, US, Singapore, and the UAE.

Prior to engaging with any seller, a thorough and rigorous external onboarding process is conducted for all our sales partners and platforms we employ.

Any products sold or services provided by Flying Sales are in full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including those related to sales, sanctions, and anti-bribery & corruption (ABAC).

Using renowned global compliance platforms, we analyze the seller, their background, and their qualifications.

Our processes are thoroughly in line with the leading standards and best practices of international companies.

We use our internally developed, digitized platform to onboard sellers. Our goal is to deliver results responsibly and reliably, upholding international standards and prioritizing health, safety, environmental, and social considerations in all our activities.

DELIVERING SUSTAINABLE SALES SOLUTIONS

We are dedicated to fostering a future where sales is both sustainable and accessible. Our strategy includes innovative practices to reduce environmental impact and promote ethical sales practices. By connecting people, ingenuity, and resources with a shared vision of value and prosperity, we aim to create a resilient sales ecosystem. This includes optimizing sales processes, investing in clean technology projects, and adhering to high environmental standards. Through these efforts, we drive sustainable growth and positively impact the global sales landscape.
